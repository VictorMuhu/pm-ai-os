# Ingestion Rules

> Rules for what can and cannot be uploaded to NotebookLM.

## Allowed source types

- Anonymized interview notes or transcripts
- Published research, reports, and articles
- Internal documents that do not contain PII or competitive secrets
- Your own analysis documents and synthesis outputs

## Not allowed

- Documents containing customer PII (names, contact info, company names without consent)
- Confidential financial data or M&A information
- Competitor internal documents obtained improperly
- Any document marked "Confidential — Do Not Distribute"

## Anonymization checklist

Before uploading customer research:
- [ ] Replace customer names with a code (e.g., "Customer A", "SMB-001")
- [ ] Remove company names if not already public
- [ ] Remove contact information
- [ ] Remove specific revenue or user count figures if confidential

## Notes

- When in doubt, anonymize before uploading
- Keep an offline log of the mapping between codes and real identities, stored securely and separately
