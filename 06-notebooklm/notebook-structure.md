## Recommended notebook structure

- One notebook per research area or initiative (not per interview)
- Upload anonymized sources only
- Tag sources on upload using the source tagging convention (see source-tagging-convention.md)
- Archive notebooks when the initiative concludes

## Notes

- Do not upload personally identifiable information (PII) or company-confidential documents
- Regularly prune stale sources to keep notebooks focused
