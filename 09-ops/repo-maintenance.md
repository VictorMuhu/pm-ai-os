# Repo Maintenance

> Guidelines for keeping the PM AI OS repos healthy over time.

## Monthly

- Review and update v1.md / v2.md roadmap progress
- Archive or delete unused templates or skills
- Check that CLAUDE.md instructions are still accurate
- Review backlog.md and promote or remove stale items

## Quarterly

- Review repo-boundaries.md — have the scopes drifted?
- Audit NotebookLM notebooks for stale sources (see ingestion-rules.md)
- Review connector-map.md and agent-boundaries.md for accuracy
- Run a demo-eval on pm-ai-demo materials if an interview is upcoming

## Ad hoc

- Update SKILL.md files after any significant usage feedback
- Add new templates when a recurring document type is identified
- Update setup-checklist.md when the setup process changes

## Archiving

- When an initiative is complete, move its artifacts to an `_archive/` subfolder rather than deleting
- Keep archived materials for at least 1 year before permanent deletion
