## What is the PM AI OS?

The PM AI OS is a personal operating system for AI-assisted product management. It connects a set of tools, workflows, templates, and skills into a repeatable system for doing high-quality PM work faster.

## Core components

- **pm-ai-skills** — reusable skill definitions (prompts + structure) for PM tasks
- **pm-ai-os** — this repo; the architecture, workflows, templates, and automation layer
- **pm-ai-demo** — sanitized demo materials for interviews and external presentations

## Design principles

- Outputs are structured, decision-oriented, and grounded in evidence
- Workflows are repeatable and documented, not ad hoc
- Templates encode best practices so quality is consistent
- AI augments judgment — it does not replace it
