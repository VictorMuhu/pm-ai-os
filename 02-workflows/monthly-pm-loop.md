# Monthly PM Loop

> Starter file. Define your monthly AI-assisted PM routine here.

## Purpose

A monthly review and planning cadence to assess progress against goals, run deeper customer synthesis, and keep the roadmap current.

## Inputs

- Monthly metrics report
- Customer research conducted this month
- Roadmap progress vs. plan
- Team retrospective notes

## Loop steps

1. **Week 1**: Run Customer Synthesis skill on any interviews or feedback from the prior month
2. **Week 2**: Review roadmap progress; update v1/v2 roadmap docs in 08-roadmap/
3. **Week 3**: Identify gaps in the plan; run Prioritization skill if new items need ranking
4. **Week 4**: Write monthly exec brief or leadership update; update metric stack if goals shifted

## AI touchpoints

- Customer Synthesis skill for monthly research consolidation
- Prioritization skill for roadmap updates
- Exec Brief skill for monthly leadership communication

## Notes

- Monthly loop should produce a refreshed roadmap view and a synthesized research summary.
